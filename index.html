About

I am a passionate and dedicated technology professional with a robust background in computational mathematics and software development. My career journey includes significant roles such as a Research Software Engineer at the Centro de Sensoriamento Remoto, where I enhance and maintain the Dinamica EGO software for modeling and simulating natural phenomena using C++ and Java. Additionally, my experience as an Undergraduate Student Researcher at the Pattern Recognition and Earth Observation Laboratory involved developing Python utilities for scientific visualization and machine performance monitoring. My academic achievements include a Bachelor of Science in Computational Mathematics from UFMG, where I honed my skills in mathematical modeling, simulation, and algorithm development, as well as a Technical Degree in System Development from COLTEC - UFMG. Driven by curiosity and a commitment to innovation, I aim to contribute to impactful projects in the tech industry.
